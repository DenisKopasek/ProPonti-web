Engineering Activity

Authorized structural engineering activities in the field of statics with a specialization in concrete structures, bridges, and pedestrian walkways. Preparation of complete drawing documentation and calculations for territorial decision, building permit, and construction realization.

Expertise Activity

Activities in the field of research projects and expert assessments of engineering structures, as well as load capacity calculations for bridges.

Diagnostic Activity

The company also conducts major inspections, diagnostics of bridges and structures, load tests, and proposals for remedial and reconstruction measures.

Analytical Activity

Structural scanning with a 3D scanner, inspections of structures and bridges using drones, as well as height work by trained climbers (diagnostics, minor repairs, crack monitoring, bearing checks, local diagnostics of prestressing, measurement of deflections, installation of strain gauges for strain measurements, ...).

Other Activity

Publishing and editorial activities. Organization of social and professional events and publishing activities.
